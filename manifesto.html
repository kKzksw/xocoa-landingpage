Collection Manifesto

More than a taste. A Statement.

Absolute Origin

Unlike industrial standards, our artisans transform the beans within the country of origin. This preserves unique volatile aromas often lost during the transport of raw beans to Europe.

Economic Justice (CSR)

By keeping the transformation local, we guarantee 4x more revenue for local economies. It is the fairest and most direct model in the industry.

Purity & Terroir

Zero Deforestation (EUDR Compliant). Zero Lecithin. Zero Artificial Flavoring. Just cacao, cane sugar, and ancestral know-how.
